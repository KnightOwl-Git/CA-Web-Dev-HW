About Me

Flexbox edition

Biography

I'm Ben, an artist in the business of making stuff move. Currently a BFA2 Student at CalArts, I’m passionate about 2D animation, experimenting with 3D tools, and compositing. My films Gary the Duck and NO DIALOGUE have been screened at film festivals like the Los Angeles Animation Festival and the New York Int’l Children’s Film Festival. NO DIALOGUE was selected for the CalArts Producers’ Show in 2023. In my free time I like making music, eductional youtube videos, and coding stuff like this website!
